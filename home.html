This website is for all those RPG fanatics like me out there who love creating and playing RPGs, as well as for those of you who are looking to get into the art of playing a role-playing games. About Me




My name's Sishir Katta, and I'm a Freshman in High School. I'm 14 years old, and I've been using HTML for about a year now. This is my first full project that I've published. I've also been playing Role-Playing Games for about 2 years now. I started off playing Dungeons and Dragons with my friends. Up until about a year ago, I had never actually run a game before. The first time I ran an RPG, it was a single-player RPG with one of my friends. I called it "Chaos Station", and it was a Sci-Fi/Horror RPG. Since then, I have run various RPGs with friends, and I've developed a pretty good understanding of how RPGs actually work. Two years ago, I also learnt HTML and CSS. Since then, I have tried to create various projects, none of which I have actually published. Most of these projects were blogs and discussion boards. None of these actually felt very significant though, and I decided that if I was going to create a website, I may as well make it about something I can relate to. I know how confusing it can be when first getting into RPGing and not knowing what the hell is going on. In the next few pages, I'll explain all of the core concepts of RPGs and Campaigns. On the fifth page, I'm going to have a blog/discussion board where I'm going to post various ideas I have for RPGs and you guys, whether you're RPG pros or newbies, can give me feedback on these ideas. On the last page, I'm going to have a discussion board where you guys can post your own ideas and everyone can collaborate to create spectacular campaigns.
